Chole Masala

1. Soak chickpeas overnight.
2. Pressure cook till soft.
3. Fry onions, ginger, garlic, and tomatoes.
4. Add chole masala and chickpeas.
5. Simmer for 15 minutes.
6. Garnish with coriander.
7. Serve hot with bhature.

Paneer Butter Masala

1. Sauté onions and tomatoes.
2. Blend into smooth puree.
3. Add butter, cream, and spices.
4. Add paneer cubes.
5. Simmer 10 minutes.
6. Garnish with kasuri methi.
7. Serve with naan.

Veg Biryani

1. Cook basmati rice till 70%.
2. Fry veggies with spices.
3. Layer rice and veggies.
4. Add saffron milk.
5. Cover and cook for 10 mins.
6. Fluff and serve with raita.

Masala Dosa

1. Soak rice and dal overnight.
2. Grind to smooth batter.
3. Ferment for 8 hrs.
4. Make dosa on tawa.
5. Fill with potato masala.
6. Serve with chutney and sambar.

Butter Chicken

1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
2. Grill or cook till brown.
3. Make creamy tomato sauce.
4. Add chicken.
5. Simmer for 15 minutes.
6. Add cream and butter.
7. Serve with naan.

Rajma Chawal

1. Soak kidney beans overnight.
2. Pressure cook till soft.
3. Make onion-tomato masala.
4. Add beans and spices.
5. Cook for 20 mins.
6. Serve hot with rice.

Pasta Carbonara

1. Boil pasta till al dente.
2. Fry bacon or veg substitute.
3. Mix eggs and cheese.
4. Combine with hot pasta.
5. Add salt, pepper.
6. Toss and serve warm.

Margherita Pizza

1. Prepare dough and rest.
2. Roll and spread tomato sauce.
3. Add mozzarella and basil.
4. Bake 10 mins at 250°C.
5. Slice and serve.

Palak Paneer

1. Boil spinach and blend.
2. Fry onions, garlic, tomatoes.
3. Add puree and spices.
4. Add paneer cubes.
5. Simmer 5 mins.
6. Serve hot with roti.

Idli Sambar

1. Soak rice and dal separately.
2. Grind and ferment overnight.
3. Steam in idli moulds.
4. Prepare sambar with dal.
5. Serve hot with chutney.
